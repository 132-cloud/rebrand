# Creative Brief — Press

**Page:** /press
**In one line:** The Press Room gives journalists, analysts, and partners approved assets, coverage, and release history in one place.

## What this page is for
A reference destination for media and analyst relations. The page serves inbound journalists (looking for quotes, boilerplate, and brand assets), outbound PR (a landing target for press releases), and partners needing official materials. Primary action: download press kit or contact the media team.

## Who it's for
Primary: Journalists, analysts, and industry reporters covering banking technology and fintech. They arrive with a deadline and need approved materials fast. Secondary: conference organizers, podcast hosts, and industry association staff needing official company information. They want accuracy and speed — not a sales pitch.

## What we're communicating
- Nymbus is a credible, media-ready company with approved assets ready to download
- All five press kit items are available: boilerplate, logos, brand guidelines, leadership assets, product overview
- Press releases are organized newest-first with date and headline
- Featured coverage signals industry visibility
- Direct media contact: media@nymbus.com — no form required

## Tone & feel
Professional and efficient. The Press Room sits in the "Built for Builders" brand chapter — it's a utility page, not a brand expression page. The tone should be organized and accessible, with the media team contact prominent throughout.

## Must-haves / non-negotiables
- media@nymbus.com contact displayed as a plain link (not hidden behind a form)
- Press kit download button — needs a real downloadable asset before launch (currently links to "#")
- Featured coverage: must use only approved publication logos with confirmed permissions — noted in code as an editorial requirement
- Press releases feed: newest first, with date, headline, and link to full release — all three current entries are placeholders; real content required before launch
- Media strip (publication logos) requires confirmed logo permissions before populating

## Interactive / visual elements
- Coverage and press release sections: list/table layout with inline "View coverage ›" and "Read release ›" link affordances
- Press kit cards: grid of 5 items, each with title and description; single "Download press kit" CTA below the grid
- Media strip: publication logos at consistent height, grayscale — requires permissions confirmation

## What to avoid
- Don't populate featured coverage or the media strip with unconfirmed publication logos or links
- Don't bury the media email — it's the primary conversion for this audience
- Avoid turning this into a company marketing page; journalists don't want to wade through product copy to find assets
- Em-dashes
