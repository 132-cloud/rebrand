# Creative Brief — Business Banking

**Page:** /solutions/business-banking
**In one line:** The Business Banking solutions page makes the case for launching business accounts — from sole proprietors to commercial clients — on the Nymbus platform without a core replacement.

## What this page is for
Mid-to-bottom-funnel for institutions evaluating whether Nymbus can support a business banking launch or expansion. The page spans three customer segments (solo operators, small businesses, commercial) and three capability themes (onboarding, payments, controls). Primary action: try the live demo; secondary action: book a formal demo via the footer CTA.

## Who it's for
Primary: CEOs and product leads at community banks and credit unions watching business deposits leave to fintechs — they want to launch a competitive business banking product without a core rip-and-replace. Secondary: commercial banking teams at existing Nymbus clients evaluating business banking expansion. They arrive with the problem clearly defined: "our business banking product can't keep commercial relationships."

## What we're communicating
- Business customers are leaving for fintechs — the urgency is real and the window is closing
- Nymbus supports the full business lifecycle: sole operators through commercial treasury teams, on one platform
- Three capability pillars: automated onboarding (KYC/KYB), money movement (ACH, wires, Positive Pay), and controls (roles, limits, approvals)
- Launch without touching the primary core — parallel deployment is always available
- Three named customer testimonials: Sara Dolan (MSUFCU), Ami Iceman (MSUFCU), Jill Castilla (Citizens Bank of Edmond)

## Tone & feel
Urgent and commercial, in the "Built Without Limits" brand chapter. Business banking audiences are more transactional than retail — lead with operational depth and controls, not consumer-style benefits. The tone should feel like a platform built by people who understand treasury operations, not just consumer apps.

## Must-haves / non-negotiables
- Live interactive demo embed (iframe of business dashboard) — this is a page differentiator; must survive the redesign
- Three-column icon features row (onboarding / payments / controls) immediately below the embed
- Three alternating feature blocks with eyebrow labels: ACCOUNT OPENING / PAYMENTS / CONTROLS
- Three-card segment grid: Solopreneurs / Small businesses / Commercial businesses
- Full FAQ (8 questions) — verbatim
- "Explore commercial banking" link to /solutions/commercial-banking/ in segment section

## Interactive / visual elements
- Business dashboard embed: /embeds/business-dashboard/index.html at 500–650px height — needs a designed replacement or polished embed for launch
- Three split-panel feature blocks: alternating text-left / text-right layout; image placeholder slots need final product screenshots

## What to avoid
- Don't flatten all business segments into one pitch — the three-tier segment structure (solo / SMB / commercial) is a key differentiator
- Don't position this as consumer banking with a business account tacked on — the commercial controls are the product
- Em-dashes
