# Creative Brief — Contact

**Page:** /contact
**In one line:** A direct-contact page for platform, partnership, and general business inquiries — not a demo request.

## What this page is for
Bottom-of-funnel conversion for visitors who want to reach Nymbus but aren't yet ready for a formal demo, or who have a non-sales inquiry (partnerships, press, general business). The form collects inquiry type to route the lead correctly. Primary action: submit the contact form.

## Who it's for
Primary: Banking executives, partners, and prospective customers with a specific question or inquiry that doesn't map cleanly to the demo flow. Secondary: press and analysts (though they may be better served by the Press page). Visitors arrive with intent — this page should get out of the way and let the form do its job.

## What we're communicating
- Nymbus is available and approachable — the headline frames it as a conversation, not a form submission
- The inquiry types signal Nymbus's range: platform, demo, partnership, growth strategy, general
- The confirmation message ("Thanks. We've received your inquiry and will get back to you shortly.") is deliberately brief and confident

## Tone & feel
Conversational and efficient. "Talk to us about your next move." sets a forward-looking tone consistent with the Built to Move brand chapter. The form should feel light and fast — not bureaucratic.

## Must-haves / non-negotiables
- Inquiry type selector (Platform / Demo request / Partnership / Growth strategy / General inquiry) — this is how leads get routed internally
- Two-column layout: copy left, form right — current implementation pattern
- Success state ("Thanks.") that confirms without over-explaining
- All required fields: first name, last name, work email, institution/company name

## Interactive / visual elements
- Form with inline validation; success state replaces form without a page reload
- Hero has the shared video background texture (footer-cta-bg.webm) — carry this through to the redesign

## What to avoid
- Don't position this as the primary demo-request path — /request-demo handles that
- No long copy before the form; the value of getting in touch is self-evident
- Em-dashes
