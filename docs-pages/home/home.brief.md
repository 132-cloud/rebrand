# Creative Brief — Home

**Page:** /
**In one line:** The Nymbus homepage orients every type of financial institution visitor and routes them toward the right platform path.

## What this page is for
Top-of-funnel awareness and consideration for banks, credit unions, and fintechs evaluating a modern banking platform. The page needs to communicate the full scope of Nymbus (core, operations, growth, AI) without overwhelming any single visitor type. Primary action: route to a product demo or expert conversation.

## Who it's for
Primary: C-suite and strategy leaders at community banks and credit unions evaluating core modernization or new digital brand launches. Secondary: product and technology leaders exploring specific platform capabilities. Visitors arrive with a defined problem — they may not yet know which Nymbus solution applies.

## What we're communicating
- Nymbus is one connected platform: core, operations, and growth — not a point solution
- The stack is holding institutions back; Nymbus closes that gap
- Proven results: #1 in live digital brand launches, 270% deposit growth, $4.4B largest community bank on modern core
- AI is built in and governed — not bolted on
- You can launch beside your core or replace it — two clear paths to market

## Tone & feel
Confident and momentum-forward, consistent with "Built to Move." The page should feel like it belongs in the Built Without Limits brand chapter — bold, structured, directional. No soft language; every section should push toward a decision or a next step. The rotating hero headline reinforces the brand's range without being scattered.

## Must-haves / non-negotiables
- Interactive prompt/chat UI in hero that routes visitors by intent — this is a key differentiator and must survive the redesign in some form
- Proof stats (four statistics) prominently placed after hero
- Two-path activation section: Launch in parallel / Replace your core
- Customer quotes (Jill Castilla, Ami Iceman, Stephen Owen) — sourced from named partners
- Labs and Managed Services must each have dedicated feature blocks

## Interactive / visual elements
- Hero: animated rotating headline ("BUILT TO MOVE", "BUILT TO WIN", etc.) and rotating placeholder in chat input — both must be preserved
- Platform bento grid: clickable capability tiles linking to sub-pages; the full-width "Launch intelligent digital banking" tile shows a live dashboard screenshot/demo embed
- Activation paths section: dark/video background with two white cards — strong visual contrast moment
- GovernedAI section: binary (0/1) flow field animation as background texture

## What to avoid
- Don't treat this as a product features page — it's a routing and framing page
- Avoid listing every capability in prose; use the bento grid and pill system for that
- No redundant CTAs in close proximity to each other
- Em-dashes in copy
