# Creative Brief — Careers

**Page:** /careers
**In one line:** A minimal talent recruitment page directing prospective candidates to open positions on Glassdoor.

## What this page is for
Awareness and conversion for job seekers. This is a thin page in the current build — a holding state with a single CTA to Glassdoor. Its job is to communicate the company's mission clearly enough to motivate the click. Primary action: click through to view open positions.

## Who it's for
Primary: Prospective employees — engineers, product managers, strategists, and banking operators considering a role at Nymbus. They arrive from LinkedIn, job boards, or direct search. Secondary: candidates referred by current employees who want a quick brand impression before applying.

## What we're communicating
- Nymbus is building the future of banking — a mission-driven place to work
- The team is made up of innovators, engineers, and strategists
- The work directly shapes how financial institutions serve communities
- Open positions are available and discoverable (via Glassdoor)

## Tone & feel
Aspirational but grounded, in the "Built for Builders" brand chapter. This page speaks to builders who want meaningful work — not a generic tech company. Keep it human and direct. The current page is a placeholder and should be substantially expanded in the redesign.

## Must-haves / non-negotiables
- Clear CTA to open positions (currently Glassdoor; may change to an ATS in future)
- Mission statement that reflects the Nymbus brand voice — not generic HR copy
- The redesign should include department or team highlights, values, and benefits — these are missing from the current page and flagged as gaps

## Interactive / visual elements
- Current page: hero + single CTA only — no additional visual elements
- Redesign should add: team photography or illustration, open role listings (ideally dynamic/API-fed from ATS), and possibly a "Life at Nymbus" culture section

## What to avoid
- Generic "we're passionate about what we do" filler
- Listing perks without context (ping pong tables, unlimited PTO) — connect benefits to the work
- Em-dashes
