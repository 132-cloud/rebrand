# Creative Brief — Platform

**Page:** /platform
**In one line:** The Platform page maps the full Nymbus technology stack — core, digital, onboarding, payments, intelligence, and AI — as one connected system.

## What this page is for
Mid-funnel consideration for technology evaluators mapping Nymbus's capabilities against their requirements. This is the most comprehensive product overview on the site — it doesn't go deep on any single capability but shows how everything connects. Primary action: book a demo; secondary action: navigate into specific product areas via carousel cards.

## Who it's for
Primary: CTOs, Chief Product Officers, and technology leaders at banks and credit unions building a platform evaluation. They arrive with a vendor matrix in mind and want to confirm coverage. Secondary: CEOs and strategy leaders who want a system-level view before a deeper conversation. They need to see the full scope, not just a point solution.

## What we're communicating
- Nymbus is one connected platform, not five vendor contracts stitched together
- Three architectural principles: real-time where it matters, API-first by design, deployed your way
- Banking Technology layer: Core, Digital Banking, Account Opening, Payments Hub, Engage, Levels
- Intelligence layer: Fraud Intelligence, Insights, Nymbus MCP, Connect
- Thomas Senecal / PeoplesBank quote anchors the proof narrative
- Two deployment paths available: primary core or parallel core

## Tone & feel
Architectural and confident, in the "Built Without Limits" brand chapter. The page should feel like a well-designed system — structured, clear, interconnected. Avoid listing features; show how the parts work together. The interactive blueprint grid and carousels signal that this is a living, modern platform.

## Must-haves / non-negotiables
- Platform architecture diagram (Platform v3.svg) as a centerpiece visual — must survive the redesign in some form
- Banking Technology carousel: 6 product cards (Core, Digital Banking, Account Opening, Payments Hub, Engage, Levels)
- Intelligence carousel: 4 product cards (Fraud Intelligence, Insights, Nymbus MCP, Connect)
- Three-icon summary row anchoring the page's architectural principles
- PeoplesBank quote (Thomas Senecal) as social proof
- Each carousel card deep-links to the relevant product page (currently /products/... routes — confirm these exist or update targets)

## Interactive / visual elements
- Platform diagram section: interactive blueprint grid with mouse-reactive grid animation (points deflect away from cursor) — this is a key differentiator visual; the final design needs an equivalent interaction
- Banking Technology carousel: horizontal scroll with prev/next controls, card snapping, shadow on hover
- Intelligence carousel: same treatment as banking tech carousel
- Both carousels: each card is 380px wide (desktop 500px), 650px tall, with a 320px image placeholder at top and copy below

## What to avoid
- Don't turn the platform diagram into a static image with no interaction — it needs to feel responsive and technical
- Avoid listing product names without connecting them to a customer benefit
- Don't duplicate the Solutions page — platform is about how the parts connect, solutions is about who they serve
- Em-dashes
