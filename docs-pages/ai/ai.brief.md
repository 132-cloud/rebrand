# Creative Brief — AI

**Page:** /ai
**In one line:** The AI page makes the case for governed, operational AI in banking — and positions Nymbus MCP as the connective tissue that makes it real.

## What this page is for
Consideration stage for banking executives and technology leaders evaluating AI strategy. The page reframes the AI conversation from agents-as-demos to governed, production-ready workflows. Primary action: book a demo or talk to an expert about the AI approach.

## Who it's for
Primary: CTOs, Chief AI Officers, and innovation leads at banks and credit unions who've seen AI demos but are skeptical about production deployment in regulated environments. Secondary: compliance and risk officers who need to understand how governance is embedded. Visitors arrive with AI interest but regulatory concern.

## What we're communicating
- A collection of AI agents is not a strategy — the connective tissue to data, approvals, and core is
- Nymbus AI is governed from within, not bolted on: permissioned, explainable, auditable
- Three adoption modes: Inform, Assist, Do — institutions choose their own pace
- Nymbus MCP connects AI to live banking data under a full audit trail
- Applied AI is infrastructure work, not demos — the difference between AI that changes operations and AI that only demos well

## Tone & feel
Confident and precise, in the "Built for What's Next" brand chapter. This is a sophisticated audience that's seen too many AI vendor pitches. Avoid hype language entirely. Lead with the hard problem (connective tissue, governance, auditability) and let the product answer it. The tone should feel like a banking operator who also understands AI architecture.

## Must-haves / non-negotiables
- "Agents are a task. Not a strategy." — this reframe is the strategic anchor of the page; must be prominent
- The Govern by Design accordion with three cards (Inside your rules / Explainable by default / Routine handled, exceptions escalated)
- Interactive visual demos for each accordion card (permissions view, audit log view, routing/triage view)
- Three-tier adoption model (Inform / Assist / Do) presented as distinct columns
- Nymbus MCP section with its own ID for deep-linking from platform page

## Interactive / visual elements
- Governed by Design section: left accordion + right live visual panel — the panel swaps when each card is selected. Three visuals needed: (1) a permissions/approval status view, (2) an audit log view with entries, (3) an incoming queue with routing decisions
- These visuals are currently built in JSX and will need to be replaced with final designed assets

## What to avoid
- No generic AI buzzwords (revolutionary, transformative, game-changing)
- Don't position this as "AI for AI's sake" — every claim must tie to a specific banking workflow
- Avoid making it sound like a compliance disclaimer page — governance is a feature, not a caveat
- Em-dashes
