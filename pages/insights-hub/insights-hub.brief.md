# Creative Brief — Insights Hub

**Page:** /insights-hub
**In one line:** A filterable content library for banking professionals covering core modernization, digital brands, AI, and managed operations.

## What this page is for
Top-of-funnel awareness and mid-funnel nurture. The Insights Hub surfaces Nymbus's expertise and point of view to banking leaders who aren't yet in a sales conversation. Content types span articles, case studies, webinars, whitepapers, podcasts, and reports. Primary action: consume content; secondary action: follow a link back into a product page or demo.

## Who it's for
Primary: Banking executives and practitioners researching modern core, digital banking, AI, and managed services — they may not be aware of Nymbus yet. Secondary: existing prospects and customers looking for supporting material. Visitors arrive with a topic in mind, not a vendor evaluation.

## What we're communicating
- Nymbus is a credible thought leader in banking modernization and digital growth
- Content is organized by type and searchable — easy to find what's relevant
- The featured story establishes recency and momentum (25 cores deployed milestone)
- Placeholder content titles reflect the actual editorial focus areas: parallel core, governed AI, managed services, account opening, PeoplesBank case study, MSUFCU

## Tone & feel
Editorial and expert, consistent with the "Built for What's Next" brand chapter. The hub should feel like a publication curated by practitioners — not a marketing content dump. Featured story treatment at the top signals that the most important piece gets editorial emphasis.

## Must-haves / non-negotiables
- Featured story module at top — large format, distinct from the grid below
- Sticky filter bar with category pills (All / Article / Blog / Case Study / Podcast / Report / Webinar / Whitepaper) and live search
- Content grid: thumbnail, category label, headline, date + read time
- Load more control — pagination or infinite scroll
- All content items are placeholders in the current build; real CMS content will populate in production

## Interactive / visual elements
- Filter bar: sticky position below global nav; active category pill highlighted; search input with clear button
- Featured story: split-panel layout — text left, image placeholder right
- Content grid: 3-column on desktop, hover state on card thumbnails (subtle scale), headline color change on hover
- No content detail pages exist yet — links currently go to "#"

## What to avoid
- Don't let filter pills break to a second row without graceful overflow (scrollable row on mobile)
- Avoid making this feel like a blog — it's a resource library with multiple content types
- Em-dashes
