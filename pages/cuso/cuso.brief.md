# Creative Brief — CUSO

**Page:** /cuso
**In one line:** A minimal landing page for Nymbus's Credit Union Service Organization offering — currently a placeholder that needs significant expansion.

## What this page is for
Awareness and lead generation for credit unions evaluating the CUSO model as a path to modern banking technology and shared services. Currently a thin holding page with a headline and two CTAs. Primary action: learn more (contact us) or request a demo.

## Who it's for
Primary: CEOs and leadership at credit unions exploring the CUSO structure as a way to access Nymbus technology and services without a full bilateral contract. These visitors have credit union-specific concerns around governance, shared services economics, and regulatory structure. Secondary: Credit union league and association staff researching the CUSO model.

## What we're communicating
- Nymbus CUSO is purpose-built for credit unions — structured as a CUSO, not adapted from a bank product
- It provides access to modern banking technology, shared services, and growth collaboration
- "Built by credit unions, for credit unions" — the framing signals community and alignment, not vendor distance

## Tone & feel
Collaborative and community-oriented — the "Built for Builders" brand chapter applied to the credit union world. Credit union audiences respond to peer language and cooperative values. Avoid corporate fintech tone; lean into the cooperative model and shared mission framing.

## Must-haves / non-negotiables
- The CUSO structure, governance model, and member benefits need to be fully explained in the redesign — the current page is a stub
- "Built by credit unions, for credit unions" tagline should survive into the final design
- Contact/learn more CTA must link to a relevant conversion path
- The redesign should add: CUSO member list or logos, governance structure overview, how it differs from a standard Nymbus contract

## Interactive / visual elements
- Current page: hero + footer CTA only — no visuals
- Redesign should add: member institution logos, possibly a diagram explaining the CUSO structure and how technology/services flow to members

## What to avoid
- Don't treat this as a general Nymbus platform page — CUSO visitors want credit-union-specific framing
- Avoid overusing the word "modern" without specifics; credit union audiences are skeptical of vendor hype
- Em-dashes
