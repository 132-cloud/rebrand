# Creative Brief — Labs

**Page:** /labs
**In one line:** Nymbus Labs is the growth team that architects, launches, and scales digital banking brands — this page sells that service to institutions ready to grow beyond the platform.

## What this page is for
Mid-to-bottom-funnel conversion for institutions that have a digital brand idea but need a growth partner, not just technology. Labs is differentiated by being wired into the Nymbus platform — strategy becomes a live bank, not a slide deck. Primary action: book a strategy session via the inline form at page bottom.

## Who it's for
Primary: CEOs, Chief Strategy Officers, and heads of digital at banks and credit unions who have the platform (or are buying it) and need the growth engine behind it. Secondary: institutions exploring their first digital brand or a new market entry and evaluating whether Labs handles the non-technology work. They arrive knowing growth is the gap.

## What we're communicating
- The platform launches the bank — Labs grows it. This is the core distinction.
- Labs is not a consultancy; it's an embedded team wired directly into the Nymbus platform
- The work covers strategy, brand, acquisition, and ongoing optimization — not just launch day
- Proven results: +459% deposits YoY across the portfolio, $50M+ in as little as 1.4 months, 97% 12-month retention
- Seven named digital brands launched: AlumniFi, Cinefi, Collegiate, Pillur, Roger, ValorFi, Zynlo

## Tone & feel
Energetic and results-oriented, squarely in the "Built for What's Next" brand chapter. Labs should feel like a growth partner you'd want in the room — confident, data-driven, and direct. The numbers section does heavy lifting; let them speak without over-explaining.

## Must-haves / non-negotiables
- Portfolio section with named brands (carousel or grid) — AlumniFi, Cinefi, Collegiate, Pillur, Roger, ValorFi, Zynlo
- Proof stats prominently placed: +459%, $50M+, 97%
- Four-step process (Define / Build / Plan / Grow) as a sequential narrative
- Inline form at page bottom: Name, Email, Company, "What do you want to grow?" textarea + "Book a session" submit
- "One difference: we are wired into the platform." — this differentiator must stay prominent

## Interactive / visual elements
- Portfolio carousel (PortfolioCarousel component) — needs final designed brand assets for each of the 7 brands
- The portfolio section is a key visual proof point; placeholder state is insufficient for launch
- The numbers section (three stat blocks) should have strong typographic treatment — large numerals

## What to avoid
- Don't let this read as generic marketing agency copy — Labs is a Nymbus-native team, not a hired agency
- Avoid burying the portfolio; it's the most credible proof point on the page
- No vague language about "strategy" without attaching it to outcomes (deposits, retention, growth)
- Em-dashes
